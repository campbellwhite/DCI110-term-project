About Me:

Hi, my name is Campbell White. I am a current senior, class of 2023, at Washington and Lee University in Lexington, VA. I will hold a Bachelor of Arts degree, with a major in Sociology and minors in Data Science and Women's, Gender, and Sexuality Studies upon graduation. I’m originally from Norfolk, VA.

I decided to add a Data Science minor rather late during my time here at W&L. In the middle of my second semester junior year my adviser asked if I had ever considered it. I admitted that I had, but that in my head, a sociology major and data science minor seemed somewhat incompatible. However, I soon leanred how naive I had been; there is certainly immense potential in the sociological field for data science skills and this realization has completely changed how I viewed my future.

Within this portfolio, I’ve included a selection of projects I have worked on throughout my time in the minor. These projects highlight my sociological interests intersecting with my interests in data science to demostrate just how useful data tools can be for this field.
