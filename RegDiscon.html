Regression Discontinuity

Scatterplot of County Poverty Rates Against High School Completion Rates

This visualization shows the bivariate relationship between county poverty rates and high school completion in respective counties. There appears to be an inverse correlation between poverty and high school completion, indicationg that as poverty decreases in a county, high school completion tends to increase. The red line in this plot reflects a cutoff point for a type of "treatment" that will be discussed later.
